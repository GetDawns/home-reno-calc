# 12-Month Build & Content Roadmap

The plan is structured around three constraints:
1. **Google takes 4–8 months to rank new sites.** Front-load content; revenue arrives after.
2. **Mediavine threshold is 50K monthly sessions.** That's the first major revenue unlock.
3. **Sustainable solo pace** ~ 4–8 articles/week + 1–2 calculators/week.

## Month 1 — Foundation
- ✅ Ship starter codebase, 5 calculators, 1 article (this repo)
- Buy the domain, set DNS, deploy to Vercel, set up `homerenocalc.com`
- Configure Google Search Console + GA4 + Microsoft Clarity (free heatmaps)
- Set up `robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml` (use Next 15 sitemap conventions)
- Apply for Home Depot, Lowe's, and Amazon affiliate accounts (HD via Impact Radius takes ~1 week)
- Build remaining 5 Tier-1 calculators (tile, gravel, lawn-seed, fertilizer, btu) → **10 total**
- Write 4 supporting articles (one per new calculator)

**Output target:** 10 calculators, 5 articles. Indexing begins.

## Month 2 — First content sprint
- Build 5 more Tier-2 calculators (fence, hardwood, laminate, carpet, wallpaper) → **15 total**
- Write 12 articles, focusing on long-tail variants ("how many bags of mulch for 200 sq ft")
- Start manual outreach for backlinks: 5 guest-post pitches per week to DIY/home blogs
- Take and upload original photos for top 5 articles (Google rewards original imagery in YMYL/E-E-A-T)

**Output target:** 15 calculators, 17 articles. First indexed pages.

## Month 3 — Build velocity
- Build 5 more calculators (insulation, roofing, siding, stair, board-foot) → **20 total**
- 16 articles. Push for one big "ultimate guide" piece (3000+ words, central pillar)
- Add internal linking automation: every article ends with "Run the numbers" section linking to relevant calculators
- Set up Pinterest as a secondary traffic source — pin every article with a custom infographic

**Output target:** 20 calculators, 33 articles total. Pinterest pinning begins.

## Months 4–6 — Push to Mediavine threshold
- Build calculators 21–35 (square-footage, brick, sand, paver, retaining wall, etc.)
- Write 60 more articles (10–12/week pace)
- First Google traffic should arrive — analyze search console weekly, double down on what's ranking
- Begin gathering email subscribers via "send me your project plan" lead magnet
- Apply to Mediavine at ~50K sessions/mo (or Raptive at 100K+ for higher RPM)

**Output target by month 6:** 35 calculators, 90+ articles. Mediavine application submitted.

## Months 7–9 — Monetization layer-in
- Mediavine ad implementation (replace `<AdSlot>` placeholders with their script)
- Optimize ad placements for RPM: in-content sticky, recipe-style anchored mobile, sidebar
- Build calculators 36–50, focusing on long-tail / specialty variants
- Write 50 more articles. Begin a YouTube companion channel (videos rank in Google results, drive traffic to calculators)
- Begin proper backlink campaign: HARO, Help A B2B Writer, niche guest posts (target 5 quality links/month)

**Output target by month 9:** 50 calculators, 140+ articles. Display revenue ~$2–6K/mo. Affiliate ~$1–4K/mo.

## Months 10–12 — Optimization & expansion
- Refresh top-30 articles with: new images, updated prices, expanded FAQs (Google rewards updates)
- Add programmatic SEO pages: "[Calculator] for [Project Type]" landing pages (e.g., "Mulch calculator for raised beds")
- Pursue brand sponsorships from paint/lumber brands (passive negotiation, $500–$5K per placement)
- Begin email newsletter with weekly DIY project ideas + calculator promotions
- Targets year-1 close-out: **50 calculators, 200+ articles, 150K+ monthly sessions, $8–15K/mo combined revenue**

## Critical milestones

- **Month 4:** First calculator hits page 1 of Google for its target keyword
- **Month 6:** Mediavine approval (~50K sessions)
- **Month 9:** $5K/mo combined revenue
- **Month 12:** Decision point — keep building solo, hire a writer, or sell

## Revenue model (realistic — not best case)

Mediavine RPM in DIY niche: **$22–35** at scale.
Affiliate: ~3–8% conversion rate × $15 avg commission per click that buys = ~$1.50 RPV.

| 100K | $2,800 | $1,800 | **$4.6K/mo** |

## What kills this plan

1. **Inconsistent shipping.** Google notices abandoned sites. Don't go more than 2 weeks without publishing.
2. **AI-only content.** Google's March 2024 update kneecapped pure-AI content. Use AI to draft; you must edit, add original photos, and add real expertise.
3. **Skipping E-E-A-T.** No author bio = no trust = no rankings. Have a real author, real photos, real about page.
4. **Generic calculators.** "Square footage calculator" exists 100× over. Your edge is **targeted long-tail with cost integration and Shop Materials affiliate panels**.
